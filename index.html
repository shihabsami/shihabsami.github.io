Education
I'm pursuing my bachelor's degree in Computer Science at RMIT University, Australia. Currently, in my final semester after three long years. Reason to choose Computer Science? I have always been fascinated by technology. My very first computer at the age of 8 opened me up to a whole new world of possibilities. Although I was primarily gaming like all kids my age, around 13, I found myself more interested in the technical side of things, specially behind the creation of games. I started learning 3d modelling and animation, but could only go so far in creating a game of own due to always looking away when it came to writing textual gibberish that gave objects life. Years later, the gibberish is what I spend most my time writing. So yeah, I am always curious to learn what makes things tick and all the more excited to be able to create things myself.
